Registry

We have set up a registry with Babylist for a few practical essentials (and maybe a few unpractical nonessentials) for our little one.

Thank you for taking a look. Gifts are completely optional and deeply appreciated.

If you are considering clothing, here are some things to consider for your search:

  • Newborn (NB): October–November — A few essentials (zip sleepers, bodysuits, hats, socks)
  • 0–3 Months: October–January — Everyday basics (long-sleeve bodysuits, footed pajamas, easy layers)
  • 3–6 Months: January–April — Transitional clothing (mix of short/long sleeves, comfy outfits, light jackets)
  • 6–9 Months: April–July — Warm-weather clothing (rompers, short sets, breathable fabrics, sun hats)
  • 9–12 Months: July–October — Lightweight outfits (shorts, tees, rompers, pajamas)
  • 12–18 Months: October–April — Layering pieces (pants, long sleeves, sweaters, light jackets)
